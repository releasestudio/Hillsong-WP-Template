Dr Stephen Crouch
Global and Australian Chair

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Stephen is a long-standing Hillsong Church member based at the Hills Campus in Sydney, Australia. He is a Kingdom Builder, and involved in pastoral care business groups, mentoring and connect group.

  • Career & Industry

    Stephen is a Charted Accountant [Fellow] and Principal of accounting and financial services firm SCA Partners. In 1996 Stephen established SCA Partners as a boutique financial services firm; a firm which focuses on delivering real wealth progress for clients. Stephen works with businesses and business owners on strategies that increase profit margins, build assets and help deliver growth.
    With over 30 years of industry experience starting as a CA with KPMG and then moving to private practice in 1996 Stephen has a wealth of commercial experience and is well placed to make contributions to strategy and structure, financial reporting and taxation, risk and assurance, supply chain and information systems. Stephen completed his Doctor of Business Administration at Macquarie Graduate School of Management in 2010.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Stephen chairs and is a director in both commercial and charitable organisations. He is a graduate of the Australian Institute of Company Directors (GAICD).

Tolu Badders
Global Board Director

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Tolu is a long-standing Hillsong Church member based at the New York City Campus in the United States. She serves across various areas and is a key part of the leadership team.

  • Career & Industry

    Tolu Badders, serves as the Executive Pastor and Chief Operating Officer at Hillsong East Coast. Tolu holds a bachelors degrees in computer science and economics and masters degrees in economics as well as regional science from Cornell University.
    She brings extensive experience and knowledge in the areas of leadership, management, strategy, and operations. She is passionate about building the church and championing people.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Tolu was appointed to the Hillsong Global Board in 2020 and is currently serving as a director.

Mrio Rui Boto
Board Director, Lead Pastor Hillsong Portugal

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Mrio is the Founder and Lead Pastor of the church that is now Hillsong Portugal, over 20 years ago. From humble beginnings with 12 people in the living room of his home to a church with thousands of people, the story of Hillsong Portugal has inspired many people, not only in Portugal, but around the globe. Passionate about building the church of Christ and raising up a new generation of followers and servants of Jesus, Mrio believes that the only way to perpetuate what God has called us to do is by raising up and sowing our own lives into future generations. Hillsong Portugal is a thriving church with over 10 locations, hosting yearly leadership, women and youth conferences that attract thousands of people.

  • Career & Industry

    Mrio is a world-renowned speaker, invited to preach and teach in churches and conferences around the world. Passionate about building the local church, empowering pastors & leaders from across the globe to fulfill Gods calling over their lives, he is highly sought-after to advise pastors about leadership transitions and growth opportunities.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Mrio has served and led in church boards for over 20 years, leading through multiple seasons of pioneering, growth and transitions. On top of that, he has also been on the board of Christian interdenominational organisations, contributing to the health and advancement of churches nationally and internationally.

Phil Dooley
Global and Australian Board Director, Global Senior Pastor

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Pastors Phil and Lucinda Dooley were appointed Global Senior Pastors of Hillsong Church in February 2023. Phil and Lucinda have each had over 30 years of ministry experience. They have established a thriving Hillsong Church in South Africa with multiple campuses and over 15,000 worshipers.

  • Career & Industry

    Before moving to Cape Town, South Africa, to plant Hillsong Church there in 2008, Phil and Lucinda had led the youth and young adult ministries of Hillsong Church Australia for 12 years. Phil was also involved in pioneering Hillsong Uniteds worship music ministry.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Phil was appointed to the Hillsong Global Board in 2016 and is currently serving as a director. (Australian Board date to be supplied by Claire H.)

Siboniso
Global Board Director

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Siboniso is a long-standing Hillsong Church member based at the Cape Town Campus in South Africa. He has served across various teams such as hosting, set up team and count room.

  • Career & Industry

    Siboniso holds a BCom (Hons); MBA (UCT) and assumed the joint leadership role within Old Mutual Equities in November 2018 and is responsible for the running of the boutique, which has over R60 billion in assets under management. Siboniso joined Old Mutual Investment Group in 2006 and was a financial analyst in the investment research team for four years.
    In 2010, he was appointed the Financial and Property Sector Head following which he was an analyst and portfolio manager in the Electus boutique. In 2015 he founded the Global Emerging Markets boutique and jointly managed the Old Mutual Global Emerging Market Fund.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Siboniso was appointed to the Hillsong Global Board in xxx and is currently serving as a director.(Australian Board date to be supplied by Claire H.)

Andreas Nielsen
Board Director, Lead Pastor Hillsong Sweden

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Andreas has over 25 years of experience across many different roles in ministry. Beginning as a worship leader, youth and college pastor in America, to being a church planter and now lead pastor. Answering the call of God, he experienced the power of Christ and the power of a healthy church community. Hillsong Sweden started small with no venues or recourses in a country where few go to church. Every step, from a handful of people to thousands has been a step in faith, trusting Gods grace and using what was in their hands. Andreas is committed to raising up a passionate generation of leaders in church and society who can build the future and carry on the great mission God has given the church.

  • Career & Industry

    Andreas worked between 1996-2000 at a church called Christ Fellowship in the US. After moving back to Sweden he worked as a youth pastor and ran a national youth movement. In 2006, he planted what is now Hillsong Sweden together with his wife Lina and a small team which has now grown to six campuses in major cities in Sweden. Andreas has travelled for over 20 years as a speaker at different churches and conferences.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Andreas has served as the chairman of Hillsong Sweden for the past 16 years. He also serves on the leadership of the Swedish Pentecostal Movement for many years.

Russell Dacre
Global Board Director, Deputy Chair of Global Board, Chair of GNC

  • Church & Spiritual Leadership

    Russ is a long-standing Hillsong Church member based at the Southwest London Campus in the United Kingdom.

  • Career & Industry

    Russ is an experienced Managing Director with a demonstrated history of working in the automotive industry. Skilled in Negotiation, Business Planning, Coaching, Sales Management, and Event Management. Strong business development professional with a Master of Business Administration (MBA) focused in Corporate Finance from the University of East Anglia.

  • Board & Governance Experience

    Russ has studied on the Chartered Director Program at the Institute of Directors (IOD) in the United Kingdom. He is a current board member of Hillsong Church London and was previously the Chairperson for both the OPEN Youth Trust from 2004-2020 and SOUL Church UK from 2016-2023.
    Russ was appointed to the Hillsong Global Board in 2016 and is currently serving as a director and deputy chairperson.
